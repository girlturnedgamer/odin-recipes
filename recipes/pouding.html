Pouding au Chomeur

Decscription

This classic French Canadian dessert highlights one of the best ingreidents around - maple syrup! Dig into this delicious gateau on a cold day for a warm treat.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 3/4 cup maple syrup, divided
  • 2 eggs

Steps

  1. Mix together 2 cups flour, 1 tsp baking powder and 1/2 tsp salt and set aside
  2. Combine 1/2 cup of milk, 1/2 cup butter, 1/4 cup maple syrup and 2 eggs until fully mixed
  3. Slowly incorporate dry ingredients, being careful not to overmix
  4. Pour mixed batter into a greased 8x5 cake pan and bake for 30-45 minutes at 375 degrees
  5. While the cake bakes, heat 1/2 cup maple syrup and 1 tbsp butter on low heat on the stovetop
  6. Remove cake from the oven and poke holes into the cake with chopsticks or a fork
  7. Remove maple syrup mixture from heat and drizzle over cake to soak completely
  8. Enjoy!
