Apple Crisp

Decscription

In my dad's house, no holiday meal is complete without this simple staple. Using Mackintosh apples, this apple crisp really brings the warm and cozy feelings to your post-meal conversations.

Ingredients

  • 8 apples
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup rolled oats
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1 tsp nutmeg
  • 1/2 cup butter

Steps

  1. Peel 8 apples, cut into 1 inch cubes and sprinkle with 1 tsp lemon juice before briefly setting aside
  2. Mix together 1 cup flour, 1 cup brown sugar, 1/2 cup rolled oats, 1 tsp cinnamon and 1 tsp nutmeg
  3. Cut 1/2 cup of butter into 1/2 inch cubes and combine with flour mixture
  4. Add apples to the bottom of a greased 8x5 cake pan and sprinkle with flour and butter mixture
  5. Bake at 350 degrees for 35-45 minutes until topping is golden brown
  6. Enjoy!
