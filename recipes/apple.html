Apple Crisp

Decscription

In my dad's house, no holiday meal is complete without this simple staple. Using McIntosh apples, this apple crisp really brings the warm and cozy feelings to your post-meal conversations.

Ingredients

  • 4-5 medium-large McIntosh apples
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup rolled oats
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1 tsp nutmeg
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup cold butter, cut into 1/2 inch cubes

Steps

  1. Peel apples, cut into 1 inch cubes and sprinkle with 1 tsp lemon juice before briefly setting aside
  2. Mix together flour, brown sugar, rolled oats, cinnamon, nutmeg and salt
  3. Combine cold butter with flour mixture to create crumble topping
  4. Add apples to the bottom of a greased 8x5 cake pan and sprinkle with crumble topping
  5. Bake at 375 degrees for 40-50 minutes until topping is golden brown
  6. Cool for 10 minutes before serving warm with vanilla ice cream
  7. Enjoy!
