Chocolate Chip Cookies

Decscription

After years and years of attempts at perfecting chocolate chip cookies, this recipe takes the cake! Sprinkling sea salt on top before baking creates the perfect sweet-salty combo to compliment the crunchy edges and soft centres of these cookies.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup butter, melted and cooled to room temperature
  • 1 1/4 cup brown sugar, packed
  • 3/4 cup white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 1/2 cups flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 3/4 tsp sea salt, extra for sprinkling
  • 1/2 cup chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Melt butter slowly in the microwave or brown on stovetop and set aside until cool
  2. Combine melted butter with brown sugar and white sugar, creaming together until white and fluffy
  3. Add one of the two eggs until combined then add other egg and vanilla extract and mix to combine
  4. Mix together flour, baking soda and sea salt in a separate bowl
  5. Add flour mixture to wet ingredients slowly to incorporate before adding 1/2 cup of chocolate chips
  6. Rest dough in fridge for at least 20 minutes while preheating the oven to 375 degrees
  7. Scoop 1/4 cup of dough onto cookie sheet, sprinkle with sea salt and bake for 10 to 14 minutes, until golden at the edges
  8. Enjoy!
